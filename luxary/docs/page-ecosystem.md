# Luxary — Page Ecosystem

## Purpose

This document defines all page types within Luxary and how they connect to create a unified luxury discovery experience.

Luxary is a content-driven luxury discovery platform.

Every page should support:

* Discovery
* Education
* Exploration
* Engagement

# Core Page Types

## 1. Homepage

Purpose:

Introduce Luxary and guide users into the ecosystem.

Primary Goals:

* Explain the platform
* Showcase featured content
* Drive exploration

Entry Points:

* Featured Brands
* Stories
* Editorial
* Experiences
* Categories

---

## 2. Category Pages

Purpose:

Organize luxury content by interest area.

Examples:

* Fashion
* Watches
* Jewelry
* Automotive
* Hospitality
* Beauty

Contains:

* Featured Brands
* Featured Stories
* Editorial Content
* Experiences

Connects To:

* Brand Pages
* Story Pages
* Editorial Pages
* Experience Pages

---

## 3. Brand Pages

Purpose:

Serve as the central destination for each luxury brand.

Examples:

* Rolex
* Louis Vuitton
* Dior
* Rolls-Royce

Contains:

* Brand Overview
* Heritage
* Timeline
* Craftsmanship
* Iconic Creations
* Related Stories
* Related Editorial
* Related Experiences

Connects To:

* Stories
* Editorial
* Experiences
* Categories

---

## 4. Story Pages

Purpose:

Deep educational storytelling.

Examples:

* The History of Rolex
* Origins of Louis Vuitton
* Evolution of Rolls-Royce

Contains:

* Story Content
* Historical Context
* Related Brands
* Related Stories
* Related Editorial

Connects To:

* Brand Pages
* Editorial Pages
* Experience Pages

---

## 5. Editorial Pages

Purpose:

Provide broader luxury insights and analysis.

Examples:

* Luxury Design Principles
* Evolution of Watchmaking
* The Future of Luxury

Contains:

* Editorial Content
* Related Brands
* Related Stories
* Related Experiences

Connects To:

* Brands
* Stories
* Experiences

---

## 6. Experience Pages

Purpose:

Host immersive content.

Examples:

* AR Experiences
* Interactive Timelines
* Virtual Tours
* Future VR Showrooms

Contains:

* Experience Content
* Related Brands
* Related Stories
* Related Editorial

Connects To:

* Brands
* Stories
* Editorial
