# Luxary — Content Strategy

## Purpose

Luxary is a luxury discovery platform.

The primary product is not commerce.

The primary product is knowledge, storytelling, inspiration, and immersive exploration.

Content is the foundation of every user experience.

Every page, interaction, and future feature should be built around meaningful luxury content.

---

# Content Mission

Help users understand:

* Why luxury brands matter
* How luxury brands were built
* What craftsmanship means
* The stories behind iconic brands
* The cultural impact of luxury
* The future of luxury experiences

---

# Content Objectives

## Educate

Teach users about luxury heritage and craftsmanship.

---

## Inspire

Create aspiration without intimidation.

---

## Build Trust

Provide authentic and credible luxury information.

---

## Encourage Exploration

Guide users toward deeper discovery.

---

## Support Immersive Experiences

Create content that can later evolve into AR and VR experiences.

---

# Core Content Pillars

## Pillar 1 — Brand Heritage

Purpose:

Explain the origin and evolution of luxury brands.

Examples:

* Founding stories
* Historical timelines
* Brand milestones
* Cultural significance

User Question:

"Where did this brand come from?"

---

## Pillar 2 — Craftsmanship

Purpose:

Reveal the expertise behind luxury products.

Examples:

* Watchmaking
* Leather craftsmanship
* Jewelry creation
* Automotive engineering

User Question:

"What makes this luxury?"

---

## Pillar 3 — Iconic Products & Creations

Purpose:

Highlight the creations that shaped brand identity.

Examples:

* Legendary watches
* Iconic handbags
* Historic automobiles
* Signature collections

User Question:

"Why is this creation famous?"

Note:

Content focuses on significance, not purchasing.

---

## Pillar 4 — Editorial Content

Purpose:

Provide broader luxury perspectives.

Examples:

* Industry insights
* Trends
* Design philosophies
* Luxury culture

User Question:

"What is happening in luxury today?"

---

## Pillar 5 — Experiences

Purpose:

Enable immersive discovery.

Examples:

* Interactive journeys
* Virtual ateliers
* AR experiences
* VR showrooms

User Question:

"Can I experience this brand differently?"

# Editorial Strategy

Editorial content should not function as a news site.

Instead it should focus on:

* Evergreen value
* Timeless luxury stories
* Educational insights
* Cultural relevance

Priority:

Timeless content over daily news.
