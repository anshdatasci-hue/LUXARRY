# Luxary — Homepage Information Architecture

## Purpose

Luxary is a luxury discovery platform focused on:

* Heritage
* Storytelling
* Craftsmanship
* Editorial Content
* AR Experiences
* Future VR Showrooms

Luxary is not:

* An e-commerce platform
* A marketplace
* A product catalog
* A luxury shopping website

The homepage exists to introduce users to the world of luxury through education, exploration, and immersive storytelling.

---

# Homepage Objectives

The homepage must:

1. Explain what Luxary is within 10 seconds.
2. Establish trust and credibility.
3. Inspire exploration.
4. Showcase luxury heritage and craftsmanship.
5. Encourage deeper engagement with brands and stories.
6. Prepare users for future immersive experiences.

2. Storytelling before technology.
3. Education before engagement.
